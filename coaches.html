Raoul Korner

25+ Years Pro Head Coach

∞ Titles & Awards

Austrian professional basketball coach with over 25 years at the highest levels of the game — spanning Germany, Austria, the Netherlands, and Taiwan.

Multiple League and Cup Championships in Austria and the Netherlands, with Coach of the Year honours throughout his career. Former Head Coach of the Austrian Men's National Team (2021 EuroBasket qualifiers).

Raoul has spent decades building and refining the offensive systems — pick and rolls, DHOs, ghost screens, spacing principles — that define how professional basketball is played in Europe today. His gift is making pro-level concepts accessible and immediately applicable to developing players.

We're thrilled to have Coach Korner return to Vancouver to share his energy, knowledge, and passion for the game with the next generation of BC players.

From Coach Korner

What you'll leave with.

Basketball is all about finding ways to create small advantages on your primary defender and drawing help from a second defender. This camp is about building that vocabulary — the same one Raoul has used to win championships across Europe.

Watch him work. Ask him directly. Leave with an understanding of the game that most players spend years trying to figure out on their own.

  • How to create an initial advantage using pick and roll
  • DHO actions and how to read the defense off them
  • Off-ball screening — timing, angles, and reads
  • Ghost and blur screens in modern offensive systems
  • Spacing, passing, and movement to sustain advantages
  • Finishing at the rim in today's professional game
