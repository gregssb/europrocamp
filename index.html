About the Camp

European pro systems.
Vancouver court.

Learn how to use some of the most important offensive elements in modern basketball to create advantages for yourself and your teammates.

Coach Raoul Korner has dedicated his life to studying and coaching basketball. With numerous championships during his 25 years as a head coach of European pro teams, Raoul is an expert in developing players and putting them in positions to succeed.

We're thrilled to have Coach Korner come back to Vancouver to share his energy, knowledge and passion for the game during an intense week of camp — where BC players get a rare chance to learn from one of Europe's top coaches.

What You'll Learn

Creating advantages.
At every level.

Basketball is all about finding ways to create small advantages on your primary defender and drawing help from a second defender. This camp will teach you exactly how players on top European teams do it — and how to keep the advantage going.

01

Pick & Roll

How top European teams use the pick and roll to create and exploit mismatches at the highest level.

02

Dribble Hand-Offs

DHO actions that generate advantages off ball movement and force defenders into uncomfortable positions.

03

Off-Ball Screens

Reading and using different off-ball screens to free yourself and create for your teammates.

04

Ghost / Blur Screens

Advanced screening actions used in modern professional systems to generate open looks and driving lanes.

05

Spacing & Movement

Proper passing, spacing, and off-ball movement to sustain and extend advantages once they're created.

06

Modern Finishes

The different finishes you need to complete plays at the rim in today's game — at any level.
